OXFORD

Oxford is a small town with a population of about 13,000 people. Due to the small population there isnt much that happens in the town. The only notable person from the town is Clara Barton who was the Angle of the Battle Field during the civil war and founded the Amercian Red Cross.

There aren't many notable places in town to go out to eat so often we will travel 20-30 minutes when we go out to eat.

There are so many different places to enjoy in Oxford with over 25 miles of hikeing trails due to flooding and nationally owned dams.

Overal not the best place to visit for a vacation but if your driving through its defently worth a stop.
